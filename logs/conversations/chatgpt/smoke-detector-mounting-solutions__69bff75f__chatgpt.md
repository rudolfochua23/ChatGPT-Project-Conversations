## User

what is the stardard outside or outer diameter of a G.I. pipe?

## Assistant

The outside diameter (OD) of a G.I. (Galvanized Iron) pipe depends on its nominal pipe size (NPS). Important: the “size” (like 1/2", 3/4") is not the actual outside diameter.

Here are the standard outer diameters 👇

🔧 Common G.I. Pipe Sizes and Outer Diameter
Nominal Size	Outside Diameter (OD)
1/2 inch	21.3 mm
3/4 inch	26.7 mm
1 inch	33.4 mm
1 1/4 inch	42.2 mm
1 1/2 inch	48.3 mm
2 inch	60.3 mm
📌 Most Common in Fire Alarm Installations

For smoke detector pipe mounting:

✅ 3/4 inch G.I. pipe

OD = 26.7 mm

This is the standard used for pendant mounts (3/4" NPT)

⚠️ Important Notes

The OD is fixed regardless of pipe thickness (Schedule 40, etc.)

Threads (NPT) are based on nominal size, not OD

When buying brackets:

Look for “3/4” NPT compatible”, not mm size

👍 Practical Tip

If you're unsure onsite:

Measure the pipe:

~27 mm → 3/4" pipe

~21 mm → 1/2" pipe

~33 mm → 1" pipe

If you want, tell me your pipe measurement (or send a photo), and I’ll confirm the exact size for you 👍
